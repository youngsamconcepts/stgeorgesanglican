About Us

ST. George's Anglican Church is a vibrant community of believers, united in faith, worship and service. Our mission is to spread the message of Christ's love and truth through worship, fellowship, and outreach programs.

We are commited to building strong relationships with each other, growing spirirtually, and making positive impact in our community and the world. Whether you're visiting for the first time or looking for a church home, we welcome you to join us on our journey of faith.
